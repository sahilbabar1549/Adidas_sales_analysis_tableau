# Adidas_sales_analysis_tableau
## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing Adidas $900M+ sales data and $332M profit performance across regions, states, products, and sales channels. The dashboard helps identify trends, top-performing areas, and business insights for data-driven decision-making.

The Product Performance Overview dashboard highlights that *Men’s Street Footwear* is the top-performing category, followed by *Men’s Athletic Footwear* and *Women’s Apparel. Lower sales are observed in **Women’s Athletic Footwear* and *Men’s Apparel*, indicating potential areas for targeted marketing and promotional strategies to improve overall performance. The analysis helps identify strong revenue drivers and categories needing strategic attention.

## Objectives

Analyze total sales and profit performance

Compare sales by region and state

Understand customer buying channels (Online, In-store, Outlet)

Identify top-selling products

Support business strategy using data insights

 ## Key Features

✔ Total Sales & Profit KPI cards

✔ Sales by Method (Pie Chart)

✔ Sales by Region (Pie & Bar Charts)

✔ Units Sold by Product (Bar Chart)

✔ Top 10 States by Sales (Tree Map)

✔ Interactive filters for better analysis
